Introduction

Technology is now a part of everyone's everyday life, but not everyone knows how the technology is made, configured, or updated in order to maintain its use. For me, I've always wondered why things are the way they are and with the help of technology, I'm able to figure out just that. But that's not enough for me. I'm not just searching for why things are the way they are, but also how everything connects with each other in order to create the bigger pictures that I see every day. Since I was very young, I've been taking things apart and analyzing them just to know how it works, and at times I would put together my own creations based on what I found. With computer science, I'm able to do this on a far greater scale, and with the collaboration of similar minded people, I believe that something extraordinary can be made.
